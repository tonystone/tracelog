FailureReason

public enum FailureReason : Error

The reason for the Writer.write(_:) failure.

See also

Writer for usage.

Available Reasons
  • unavailable

    The Writer is unavailable for writing at the moment but can recover.

  • error(_:)

    The writer failed to write (unrecoverable).
